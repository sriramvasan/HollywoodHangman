How to play the game?

Rules of the Game:

  1. All the blanks are names of famous movies.
  2. You have 9 lives in total.
  3. You should enter one letter at a time inside the given textbox.
  4. Each wrong guess costs you one life.
  5. When the lives run out the game ends and redirects you to the start page.
  6. You can also guess the movie name by typing all the characters at once (of the given word length).
    • If all the characters entered are correct then you get maximum score and win the game.
    • If all the characters are entred at once and not correct then all the 9 lives are depleted and game is ended.
